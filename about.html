Frequently Asked Question

How do I book a hotel through voice commands?

Simply start by saying, "Book a hotel," followed by your desired location and dates. Our system will guide you through the available options and confirm your booking.

How do I know if a hotel has availability?

Our system automatically checks for availability in real-time when you inquire about a hotel. You'll see available rooms instantly on the screen, and they will be mentioned in the voice prompt as well.

Is it possible to see the room before booking?

Absolutely! We provide virtual tours of the rooms, which you can view directly on our platform before finalizing your booking.

Can I book multiple rooms at once?

Yes, you can book multiple rooms in a single transaction. Specify the number of rooms when you make your booking, and we'll show you all available options.

How do I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team via the 'Contact Us' section on our website or by saying, "I need help" during your booking process.
